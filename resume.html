Research and Development, Shelfie—2014-

Shelfie was an app that automatically identified books in photos that our users took of their bookshelves. I wrote most of the automatic recognition system, including feature-based nearest-neighbor matching, text-recognition, and convolutional neural networks. I created custom validation datasets and tools for quality control. I managed our SRED and IRAP projects, drafted patent applications, and mentored teammates when they helped with this research. The tech and team is now part of Kobo.
